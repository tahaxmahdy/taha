Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you specialize in?

I specialize in all major advertising platforms including Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok, and Snapchat. I have extensive experience creating and optimizing campaigns across these platforms to maximize ROI.

How do you measure campaign success?

I measure campaign success using key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Return on Ad Spend (ROAS), Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), Click-Through Rate (CTR), Conversion Rate, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV). These metrics help me optimize campaigns for maximum effectiveness.

What is your approach to budget allocation?

My approach to budget allocation is data-driven and flexible. I start with a test phase to identify the best-performing platforms, audiences, and creatives. Based on the results, I reallocate budget to maximize ROI, continuously monitoring and adjusting as needed.

How often do you report on campaign performance?

I provide weekly performance reports with key metrics and insights. For clients with larger budgets or more complex campaigns, I also offer bi-weekly or monthly strategy calls to discuss performance and optimization opportunities.

What is your typical client onboarding process?

My onboarding process begins with a discovery call to understand your business goals, target audience, and previous marketing efforts. I then conduct a thorough audit of your current advertising accounts and develop a customized strategy. Once approved, I implement the strategy and begin continuous optimization.
